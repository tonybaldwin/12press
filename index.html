Welcome 12Press.

12Press is a FREE/Open Source online meeting and event schedule presentation platform designed for 12 Step recovery fellowships (NA, AA, etc.) and similar organizations written in PHP with a MySQL (or MariaDB or Percona) database backend, released according to the GNU/Affero License
